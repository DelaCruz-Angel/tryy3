About SafeRouteManolo

Who We Are

SafeRouteManolo is a community-driven platform focused on keeping everyone safe on the roads of Manolo Fortich. We provide real-time updates on road hazards, traffic disruptions, weather conditions, and other important alerts. Our goal is to give drivers, commuters, and pedestrians the information they need to travel with confidence and awareness.

By combining user reports, on-site observations, and smart data tools, SafeRouteManolo serves as a reliable guide for anyone navigating local routes. We believe that informed communities lead to safer roads for all.

Features

Stay updated with our interactive dashboard, where you can view all recently added markers or posts submitted by the community. Each post includes details such as hazard type, location, description, and severity level.

The dashboard also includes a live weather display for Manolo Fortich, giving you real-time temperature, conditions, and forecasts to help you prepare for your travel.

Additionally, a severity chart visually represents the level of each reported hazard—allowing you to quickly understand which areas require caution or immediate avoidance.

Explore our interactive map to see hazard markers placed across specific locations in Manolo Fortich. Each marker is clickable and provides complete information about the hazard, including images, descriptions, and severity ratings.

Users can also contribute by adding new markers or posts directly on the map. Simply tap a location, upload details, and your report will appear instantly—helping others stay safe.

Whether you're planning a route or checking conditions ahead of time, the map ensures you always have a clear and accurate view of road safety conditions.

Our Mission

Our mission is to enhance public safety by delivering accurate, timely, and community-verified information about road hazards across Manolo Fortich. We aim to reduce accidents and improve route awareness for all travelers.

By empowering users to report issues and stay updated, we promote a culture of shared responsibility—one where everyone contributes to making our roads safer, more efficient, and more predictable for daily travel.

SafeRouteManolo seeks to bridge technology and community cooperation to create a safer environment for every driver, commuter, and pedestrian.
